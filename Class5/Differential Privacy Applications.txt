
Privacy Applications in Machine Learning:

1. Federated learning

	- without transfering the data to central server (google gboad keyboard)

2. Secure Multi-Party Computation

	- example, multiple banks collaborate with each other for fraud detection but make to not share a single user data 
	  with any other company to make sure the privacy of user

3. Homomorphic Encryption:
	
	- In healthcare, encrypting the patient data before sending to the third part such model to perform analysis
          & getting insights in the form of ecrypted data, At the end, we have decrypt the results to see it for privacy 
          of patient data.

4. Privacy-Preserving Data Publishing: 

	- Publishing any user's data to social media without revealing their name.
